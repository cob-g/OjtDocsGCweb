Reflections & Insights

My OJT Journey

My On-the-Job Training at GC WEB EXPERTS transformed my theoretical knowledge into practical expertise. The real-world challenges pushed me beyond my comfort zone, revealing the true nature of professional development.

Collaboration and communication became my most valuable lessons. Unlike academic projects, professional development requires constant coordination with teams, stakeholders, and clients - skills no textbook could teach.

Every challenge — from debugging complex issues to tackling real-world tasks — helped me grow as a developer and problem solver. The mentorship I received was truly valuable, and I look forward to paying it forward in the future.

Technical Skills Gained

  • • Gained a solid understanding of how development is structured within the WordPress ecosystem
  • • Learned the true principles and process behind effective web design
  • • Built foundational knowledge in product planning and management
  • • Experienced how developers collaborate through organized and efficient workflows

Professional Skills Developed

  • • Clear and efficient exchange of technical information
  • • A flexible, step-by-step way to manage projects and adapt as you go.
  • • Pushing yourself to use time wisely and finish what needs to be done.
  • • Staying calm and solving problems when things get stressful.
